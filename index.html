Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AM Softs ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use our mobile game (the "Game"). The Game is intended for a general audience, including children (7+), and contains fantasy-style combat (cartoon/fantasy fighting, not realistic violence).

Summary: We don’t require accounts and we don’t ask for personal details like your name, phone number, address, or photos. Some limited data may be collected automatically for ads, fraud prevention, and app stability (for example, crash logs).

1. Information We Collect

We do not require account registration, and we do not ask players to provide personal details such as a name, phone number, address, or photos.

To support ads and maintain the Game, certain information may be collected automatically by the Game and our advertising partner:

  • Device and app information: device model, operating system version, app version, language, and basic diagnostics (such as crash logs).
  • Approximate location: derived from IP address for general purposes such as ad delivery and fraud prevention (not precise GPS).
  • Ad interaction data: ad impressions, clicks (if any), and related performance measurements.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Serve and display ads in the Game.
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud.
  • Improve Game stability and fix bugs (for example, crash analysis).
  • Comply with legal requirements.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process information to show ads, measure performance, limit frequency, and detect invalid traffic or fraud.

Because the Game may be used by children (7+), we aim to use age-appropriate ad settings and show non-personalized ads for users who are children or under the applicable age of consent.

Google privacy and advertising information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising & Ads Technologies

4. Personalized vs Non-Personalized Ads

Where required by law (for example, EEA/UK), consent may be requested for personalized ads. If consent is not provided, non-personalized ads may still be shown.

You can also control ad personalization from your device settings (options vary by Android version): Settings → Google → Ads.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information:

  • With Google AdMob to serve ads, provide reporting, and prevent fraud.
  • With service providers that help operate the Game (for example, hosting or diagnostics), if used.
  • With legal authorities if required to comply with law or to protect users and the integrity of the Game.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

7. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy (ads, security, diagnostics, and legal compliance). Advertising partners may retain data according to their own policies.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised effective date.
